Our Approach

Honest About AI.
Deliberate About Where It Goes.

Most AI agencies sell hype. We sell clarity — on real cost, and on where automation actually belongs.

We Know the Real, Hidden Costs of AI

A chatbot demo is cheap. A production AI system that stays accurate, stays online and stays compliant is not. Model usage, retraining, monitoring, data cleanup, integration debt — these are the hidden costs most agencies never mention until the invoice arrives. We scope them upfront, so the automation you buy is the automation you can actually afford to run.

Transparent Cost of AI

AI Where It Counts — Not AI Everywhere

We don't automate a business end to end just because we can. We look for the repetitive, low-value work — the missed calls, the copy-pasted answers, the after-hours silence — and hand that to AI. The time it frees up goes back to your team, for the work that actually needs a human: judgment, relationships, craft. Done right, AI creates room for people, not fewer of them.

06 · FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything about MyBetty, GRAAL and working with Spectra Media AI.

What is Spectra Media AI?

Spectra Media AI is a premium AI web studio. We build modern websites powered by conversational AI that capture and qualify leads automatically — and we ship our own products: MyBetty AI (a 24/7 AI assistant for France and the US) and GRAAL (an AI that turns a business idea into a live application).

What is MyBetty and how much does it cost?

MyBetty is an AI professional character that welcomes your website visitors 24/7, answers their questions and captures qualified leads (name, email, phone, need). It's €179/month in France and $149/month in the US, each with a 7-day free trial and no credit card. Optional turnkey setup: +€99 / +$99.

What is GRAAL?

GRAAL is a business-idea generator that builds your AI application automatically. You describe your context and constraints, and GRAAL finds a profitable idea, generates the full web application and puts it online — with no Vercel, Stripe or GitHub setup. Available to pre-order at €49 (one-time), shipping September 2026.

Which platforms does MyBetty work on?

MyBetty works on WordPress, Wix, Webflow, Squarespace and plain HTML sites. It installs with a single snippet, supports multiple sites, includes an analytics dashboard and is GDPR compliant.

Is my data GDPR compliant?

Yes. MyBetty is built privacy-first with encrypted data storage and is GDPR compliant in both France and the US. Lead data is only ever used to connect you with your prospects.

How fast can you deploy?

MyBetty can be live on your website within 24 hours. A fully custom AI website is delivered in about 72 hours from brief to launch.

What are the hidden costs of AI automation?

Beyond the initial build, production AI systems carry ongoing costs most agencies don't disclose upfront: model usage, retraining, monitoring, data cleanup and integration maintenance. We scope these costs before you commit, so there are no surprise invoices once your system is live. See our full approach.

Does Spectra Media AI automate everything with AI?

No. We place AI only where it removes genuinely repetitive, low-value work — missed calls, copy-pasted answers, after-hours silence — and leave the rest to people. The goal is to free your team's time for judgment, relationships and craft, not to replace them with AI everywhere.
